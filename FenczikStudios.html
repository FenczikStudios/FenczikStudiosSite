2D ANIMATION

Oakview Mechanical is a local HVAC company based in Colorado Springs. They tasked me with creating an iconic mascot for their brand and a commercial that true stands out from the crowd. The result was a hand-drawn traditional animation animated in photoshop and composited in after effects. The Backgrounds were created in Blender to provide extra speed, quality and continuity to the project. This commercial was a joy to make. If you are interested in a commercial like this one or are interested about the process. Feel free to contact me directly!

3D ANIMATION

Tectum Roofing is a local Roofing Company in Colorado Springs. Tectum already has a bold and exciting brand identity that set them apart from the rest. However, they were looking to take it up a notch by bringing their mascot into THE THIRD DIMENSION! Using, blender ray tracing and PBR(physically based rendering) materials I was able to create amazing, striking visuals in a short time! After I built, the character I textured him and added materials. Then I placed him into the scenes and created the lighting. After the look of the commercial was fleshed out, I rigged and animatged the character. All post-processing, like the holograms were created in blender then composited using after effects

GAME ANIMATION

I have been calling this character "Shotgun Hippo" for lack of a better name. He was created using using spine2d animation software and is intended to be an enemy in a scifi action beat-em-up. Using spine2d for game and app animation allows me to have an incredible flexiblilty with animations. Spine2d allows for things like dynamically blending between multiple animations and much more!

This character i made to be a player character in the same sci-fi action beat-em-up as Shotgun Hippo. Guitarbacus App

The Guitarbacus is an innovative new Guitar learning application I am developing for web and mobile devices. The Guitarbacus acts as a skeleton key for the guitar and it's an idea that is very close to my heart. I have always dreamed of creating a simple tool that allows musicians to learn the guitar, music theory and improvisation in a new intuitive way. The Guitarbacus is designed to maximize creativity. And I truely believe it does just that!

Click on to learn more!

Comprehensive guides and tools accompany the Guitarbacus app to heighten the learning experience. At the click of a button the user will be able to access guides filled with chords and music theory to expand your ideas of what you can do with the Guitaracus. Click the sound button to play any note on the guitarbacus to providing audio confirmation for every new concept. The Guitarbacus website will also be a vital component to the overall Guitarbacus experience. Utilize forums to engage with the Guitarbacus community. Share new musical discoveries or ask the experts for help!

Jazzman's Quest

Jazzman's Quest is a puzzle platformer game in early development. Theo Jazzman finds himself mysteriously tranported into the Jazz Realm. A mysterious place with seemingly no rhyme or reason. Explore the many bizarre levels, rethink what is possible to solve puzzles and make your way from door to door. And create interesting avant-garde music while you play!

Jazman's quest is a love letter to the abstract constructionist movement as well as the fusion jazz movement. Google abstract constructionism, play an album like Miles Davis' Bitches brew on Spotify and just like that you will be transported into the Jazziverse! The only thing left is to add mind bending puzzles and interactive musical objects. Put all these elements together and you have Jazzman's Quest. I wonder what kind of mysteries await Theo throughout his journey.

This project is still in development, sign up for the Fenczik Studios email-list to get progress updates for Theo's Quest!
