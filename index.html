Coding & Development

The Guitarbacus is an innovative new Guitar learning application I am developing for web and mobile devices. The Guitarbacus acts as a skeleton key for the guitar and it's an idea that is very close to my heart. I have always dreamed of creating a simple tool that allows musicians to learn the guitar, music theory and improvisation in a new intuitive way. The Guitarbacus is designed to maximize creativity. And I truely believe it does just that!

Click on to learn more!

Comprehensive guides and tools accompany the Guitarbacus app to heighten the learning experience. At the click of a button the user will be able to access guides filled with chords and music theory to expand your ideas of what you can do with the Guitarbacus. Click the sound button to play any note on the guitarbacus and provide audio confirmation for every new concept. The Guitarbacus website will also be a vital component to the overall Guitarbacus experience. Utilize forums to engage with the Guitarbacus community. Share new musical discoveries or ask the experts for help!

This project is still in development, sign up for the Fenczik Studios email-list to get progress updates for The Guitarbacus App!
