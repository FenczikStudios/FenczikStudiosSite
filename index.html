About the Artist

Hi! I'm Steven Fenczik, founder of Fenczik Studios LLC.
I've had a passion for all things creative for as long as I can remember. In 2023, after a decade of freelancing in art and design, I decided to leverage my diverse skill set and establish Fenczik Studios LLC. At Fenczik Studios, we specialize in crafting custom logos, responsive websites, unique merchandise, and eye-catching animations for small businesses eager to stand out and grow. I'm excited to learn about your amazing business and explore how Fenczik Studios can help bring your vision to life.
