Steven Fenczik is a freelance artist and animator with experience working in both 2D and 3D environments and workflows. He graduated from The University of Colorado Boulder in 2016 with a degree in Studio Art. Over the last decade, he has created a wide variety of digital and print assets from logos and box designs to animated commercials and motion graphics. This site, as well as everything you see on it was created by Steven.

Guitarbacus.com is a guitar-learning application I am developing for web and mobile devices. The goal of guitarbacus.com is to provide a unique way of learning guitar and music generally with an emphasis on improvisation and creative expression. The main page of the site allows users to select any key and musical mode they like and see the scale appear immediately on the guitarneck. For beginners, I have written guides inside the app to help introduce all the necessary information required to start making music like a pro!

The site is still under construction, however I would love to hear your thoughts! Feel free to spend some time with the guitarbacus, then send me a message
