    Guitarbacus.com is a guitar-learning application I am developing for web and mobile devices. The goal of guitarbacus.com is to provide a unique way of learning guitar and music generally with an emphasis on improvisation and creative expression. The main page of the site allows users to select any key and musical mode they like and see the scale appear immediately on the guitarneck. For beginners, I have written guides inside the app to help introduce all the necessary information required to start making music like a pro!
